Getting you high since 1921.

The Strathclyde University Mountaineering Club has been causing mayhem on the Munros for over a century.

From weekend trips to all the most spectacular mountain areas Scotland has to offer, to challenging yourself on a climbing wall during our weekly sessions, to simply seeing how quickly you can finish a beer at our weekly socials, SUMC has something for everyone.

Whether you’re a seasoned climber or a curious beginner, SUMC welcomes all.

Club members are able to join us on every trip or day out, access our incredible social events and borrow any mountaineering kit they need from the club kit store!

Need more info? Find out below!

Mountaineering

From hiking to climbing and general shenanigans, we have multiple trips per term (official and unofficial) to the beautiful Munros. Whether you’re looking to conquer a challenging peak or simply seeking a moment of serenity in nature, the weekend trips offer opportunities for all!

Climbing

Push yourself to your limits at our weekly climbing sessions. Whether you’re honing your technique or simply looking to laugh at your friends falling off the wall, our weekly climbing sessions are perfect for you.

Socials

When we aren’t scrambling about on the Munros, we like to kick one back at our weekly socials. If you want to meet people prior to running up a Munro with them, the best way is to join one of our chaotic events, like our ceilidhs or sub crawls.
